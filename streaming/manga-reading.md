## 2. WeebCentral

- **Website:** https://weebcentral.com
- **Content:** Manga, manhwa (Korean), manhua (Chinese), webtoons
- **Account Required:** No for reading, optional for bookmarks and history

### Overview

WeebCentral is a manga aggregator and reader that provides a large catalog of manga chapters sourced from scanlation groups and official translations. It covers ongoing series with regular chapter updates as well as completed works.

### Key Features

- **Large catalog:** Thousands of manga titles spanning all major genres (shonen, shojo, seinen, isekai, romance, horror, sports)
- **Fast updates:** New chapters added quickly after Japanese release
- **Manhwa and manhua:** Not just Japanese manga — extensive Korean and Chinese comic coverage
- **Multiple reading modes:** Left-to-right (webtoon/manhwa), right-to-left (manga), long-strip vertical scroll
- **No account needed:** Read immediately without signing up
- **Bookmark and history:** Optional account to track progress across devices
- **Mobile-friendly:** Works on mobile browsers without an app install

### Content Categories

| Type | Origin | Reading Direction | Examples |
|------|--------|------------------|---------|
| **Manga** | Japan | Right to Left | One Piece, Jujutsu Kaisen, Berserk |
| **Manhwa** | Korea | Left to Right (vertical) | Tower of God, Solo Leveling, Noblesse |
| **Manhua** | China | Left to Right | Battle Through the Heavens, Doupo Cangqiong |
| **Webtoon** | Korea/Global | Vertical scroll | Lore Olympus, True Beauty |

### Navigation

```
Homepage → Featured / Latest updates / Popular
Browse → By genre, type (manga/manhwa/manhua), status (ongoing/completed)
Search → Title search, author search
Latest → Chronological new chapter updates
```

### Reading Interface

WeebCentral's reader supports:
- **Page-by-page:** Classic manga reading, tap/click sides to advance
- **Long strip:** Continuous vertical scroll (best for webtoons and manhwa)
- **Zoom:** Pinch to zoom on mobile, scroll to zoom on desktop
- **Preloading:** Next pages load in background for smooth reading
- **Chapter navigation:** Jump between chapters without returning to series page

## 3. Other Notable Platforms

### MangaDex
- **Website:** https://mangadex.org
- **What it is:** Community-driven manga hosting platform where scanlation groups officially upload their translations
- **Strengths:** Highest quality translations, multiple language support (English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, and 30+ more), no ads, chapter comments
- **Account:** Optional for reading, required for bookmarks
- **Downloads:** Yes — MangaDex allows chapter downloads through its interface

```
MangaDex features:
- Official home of many major scanlation groups
- Multi-language support per chapter
- Reading history without account (browser local storage)
- MDList for reading tracking
- Follow groups and authors
```

### Tachiyomi (Android)
- **Website:** https://tachiyomi.org (app, not a website)
- **What it is:** Open-source Android manga reader that connects to dozens of sources including MangaDex, WeebCentral, and 100+ other sites via extensions
- **Key feature:** Download chapters for offline reading
- **No ads:** Clean reading experience
- **Note:** Tachiyomi discontinued officially in 2024 — active forks include **Mihon** (https://mihon.app) which is the direct continuation

```bash
# Install Mihon (Tachiyomi successor) on Android
# Download APK from https://mihon.app/download
# Add extension sources after install:
# Browse → Extensions → install sources you want (MangaDex, WeebCentral, etc.)

# Features:
# - Download chapters for offline reading
# - Track reading progress (syncs with AniList, MAL)
# - Categories for organizing your library
# - Multiple reader modes per series
```

### Cubari
- **Website:** https://cubari.moe
- **What it is:** Open-source manga reader that works as a front-end for imgur albums and other image hosting
- **Strength:** Privacy-focused, no tracking, simple clean reader
- **Use case:** Reading manga uploaded as image dumps to imgur

## 4. Legal Alternatives

| Platform | URL | Price | Content |
|----------|-----|-------|---------|
| **Viz Media** | viz.com | Free (limited) / $2.99/mo | Shonen Jump manga — official |
| **Manga Plus** | mangaplus.shueisha.co.jp | Free | Official Shueisha releases (One Piece, Naruto, etc.) |

| **Webtoon** | webtoon.com | Free | Official webtoons, Originals |
| **Tapas** | tapas.io | Free (coins for premium) | Webtoons and indie manga |

**Manga Plus** (https://mangaplus.shueisha.co.jp) deserves special mention — it's the official free platform from Shueisha (publisher of Weekly Shonen Jump) providing the first 3 and latest 3 chapters of all their series for free, with full series available to subscribers. Major titles like One Piece, My Hero Academia, and Chainsaw Man are available here legally and for free.

## 6. Usage Tips

**Ad blockers:** Like anime streaming sites, manga aggregators serve ads. uBlock Origin in Firefox/Chrome significantly improves the reading experience.

**Mihon for offline reading:** If you use Android, Mihon (Tachiyomi fork) is by far the best reading experience — downloads chapters for offline, clean interface, no ads, syncs with AniList.

**MangaDex for quality:** When translation quality matters or you want a specific language, MangaDex has the best scanlation group coverage and multiple translation options per chapter.

**Legal reading where possible:** For currently running Jump series, Manga Plus provides the latest chapters legally and for free within hours of Japanese release. Reading there directly supports the authors.

**Tracking your reading list:** Use **AniList** (anilist.co) or **MyAnimeList** (myanimelist.net) — both have manga tracking sections. Mihon syncs directly with AniList.
